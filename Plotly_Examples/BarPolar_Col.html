Using a polar bar chart makes it easy to see categories if you use color. Here, I plotted water depth (r) for each month of 2021 to see general ups and downs of the Colorodo river. We can see that the first 5 month (Jan - May) is generally wet. However, as summer comes along and winter, the river dries up. A bar polar chart is great at showing big differencecs in number using the size of their bar charts. However, in my dataset, water level change gradually, meaning a limitation is I won't be able emphasize the bar size as much.
