The two year composites show that there is development of housing and industry in the Pineville area of Charlotte. This three year composites show a more refined timescale of development. Simply from the two year composite, I assumed that developement of homes occur after industries flow in because new homes are needed for new workers. At least in this snapshot it seems as though houses came first before industries. This doesn't necessary mean new homes bring industry because this is a small portion of Charlotte. Industry might have developed in other areas of Charlotte not captured in the photo and the flux of neighborhood development captured is the result.
